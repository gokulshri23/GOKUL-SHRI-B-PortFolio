about-me

Learning to think in data.

I'm Gokul Shri B, currently pursuing my B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science at Saranathan College of Engineering. My interest sits at the point where raw numbers turn into a decision — cleaning a dataset, spotting the pattern in it, and building something (a model, a script, a small app) that puts that pattern to use.

I'm still early in my journey, which is exactly why I like it: every dataset teaches me something new about Python, and every small project pushes my understanding of data structures a little further. I care about writing code that's clear enough for someone else to read, and analysis that's honest about what the data actually shows.

Outside of coursework, I'm building the habit of documenting what I learn and pushing it to GitHub — this portfolio itself is part of that practice.
